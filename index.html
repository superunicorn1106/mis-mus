Unicorn Deserts

Every once in a while, we love those rainbow horned creatures. Sometimes you even wish what it would be like if you became one. One way you can become a unicorn is to eat unicorn food! Read on to find out how to make unicorn deserts!

Uni Shake

-Any type of ice cream

-1 cup of strawberries

-Whipped cream

-Toppings to make it more magical

First start off with strawberries and blend them in a blender until it is mushy. Mix the strawberries with ice cream and set them in the freezer for 30 minutes.

Unicorn Ice cream

-2 cups of whipping cream

-14 ounces of condensed milk

- 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

- Gel food coloring

- Eggs

In a large mixing bowl, beat the whipping cream. Pour the can of condensed milk and vanilla extract into the whipping cream and mix. Seperate the base into 5 bowls and add 2-4 drops of food coloring into each bowl. Use a loaf pan and layer. Place 1/2 of each color
